Solar system


Our Solar system is made up of Sun and all the amazing objects that travel around it the solar system is gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly.The largest are the eight planets,with the remainder being small object ,dwarf planet and small Solar System bodies.Here we are providing a brief information of planets of Solar System .it will be easy to remember and develope your intrest towards space science.

Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System,The Sun is classified as a G2 V star
comprises about 99.86% of the mass of the Solar System
The Sun is composed primarily of the chemical elements hydrogen and helium
Its diameter is 109 times that of Earth
its mass is about 330,000 times that of Earth
